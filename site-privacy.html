Website Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for snokkk.github.io

Last updated: 2026-04-04

This Website Privacy Policy explains how Snow Bat handles information when you visit snokkk.github.io. It applies to the website itself, including the homepage, support pages, changelog pages and other pages published under this domain.

This policy does not apply to our games, game backends, in-game accounts, or third-party stores and platforms where our games are distributed. The separate game privacy policy is available at Game Privacy Policy.

What information we process

  • We do not create user accounts, do not offer checkout, and do not run first-party advertising or analytics scripts on this website.
  • Like any hosted website, our hosting provider may process standard technical request data needed to deliver pages and protect the service. This can include IP address, browser and device data, requested URL, referrer, and access time.
  • If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message. We use that only to reply and handle your request.
  • Some pages on this site load the Google Translate widget. When that widget is loaded or used, Google may receive technical data and may store translation preferences according to its own policies.
  • If you click links to external services such as Google Play, the App Store, itch.io, Playgama, Yandex Games, Discord, Telegram, YouTube or similar platforms, those services process data under their own privacy notices.

How we use information

  • To make the website available and secure.
  • To respond to support or business emails.
  • To provide optional translation functionality on selected pages.
  • To investigate abuse, availability issues, or suspicious traffic if needed.

Cookies and similar technologies

We do not use first-party analytics or advertising cookies on this website. However, third-party services loaded on specific pages, especially Google Translate, may use cookies or similar storage. Details are described in our Cookie Policy.

Legal bases

Where data protection laws such as the GDPR apply, we generally rely on our legitimate interests to operate, secure, and maintain the website. Where you choose to contact us, use a third-party widget, or open third-party platforms, processing may also be based on your request or your interaction with that service.

Sharing and international transfers

We do not sell your personal data. Limited technical data may be processed by service providers that host or deliver the website or provide optional third-party functionality. Because those providers may operate globally, data may be processed outside your country.

Retention

  • Email correspondence is kept only as long as needed to answer you and maintain support records.
  • Technical logs are retained by our hosting or third-party providers under their own retention schedules.
  • We do not build visitor profiles or maintain website user accounts.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, deletion, restriction, or objection. Because this website does not maintain visitor accounts, our ability to identify a specific visitor may be limited, but you may still contact us and we will review your request in good faith.

Children

This website is intended for a general audience and is not designed to knowingly collect personal data from children through forms or account registration.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
