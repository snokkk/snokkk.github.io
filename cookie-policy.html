Cookie Policy

Cookie Policy for snokkk.github.io

Last updated: 2026-04-04

This Cookie Policy explains how Snow Bat uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. We keep cookie usage minimal and do not use first-party advertising or analytics cookies on the site.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files or similar browser storage records that help websites or embedded services remember settings, improve delivery, or protect against abuse.

How we use cookies

  • Our own pages do not set cookies for marketing, profiling, or first-party analytics.
  • Some informational pages on the site load the Google Translate widget: changelog.html, troubleshooting.html, roadmap.html and roadmap_ru.html.
  • When Google Translate is loaded or used, Google may place cookies or use similar browser storage to remember the selected language, maintain service preferences, secure the service, and improve delivery.
  • Those cookies or storage records are controlled by Google, not by Snow Bat.

External links

Links to Google Play, the App Store, Discord, Telegram, YouTube, itch.io, Playgama, Yandex Games and other external platforms do not set cookies on our site just by being displayed as normal links. If you open those services, their own websites may use cookies under their own policies.

Managing cookies

  • You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can block third-party cookies or ask the browser to request permission before storing them.
  • If third-party cookies or browser storage are blocked, the Google Translate widget may not work correctly or may not remember your selected language.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always appear on this page with an updated date.
