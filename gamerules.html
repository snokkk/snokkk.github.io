Game and chat rules

Game and Chat Rules (English)

Brief game rules:

1. Prohibited: profanity, insults, sexual content, advertising, spam, warez, cheats, politics and extremism.

2. Players under 14 years old can only use quick chat (fixed phrases, no free text).

3. For violating the rules — temporary or permanent account suspension.

4. How to report: «Pause → Report player» or send an email to snowbatstudio@gmail.com / Discord / Telegram.

5. Chat history is stored for 12 months; all messages are public and reviewed by moderators.

6. The Game Administration is not responsible for the content of player messages.

Full text of the game and chat rules:

This text regulates the rules of participation and behavior of Players in the Game Chats, restrictions on Player actions, and the responsibility of Players for non-compliance with these rules.

Using the Game means the Player fully agrees with these Rules.

The participation and behavior rules are designed to make being on the Game resources as comfortable and safe as possible. Failure to comply may result in restriction of access to the Player’s account without compensation.

Definitions

Flood — low-content or repetitive chat messages.
Flame — quarrels, provocations, insults, discrimination.
Warez — discussion and/or distribution of unlicensed content (“cracks”, noCD, serials, hacks, etc.).
Administration — representatives of the Licensor responsible for the operation of the Game and maintaining order in it.

Types of blocking in the Game Chat:

Read Only — partial blocking: you can read chats but cannot send messages.

Account freeze / Ban — complete suspension from the Game and Chat for a period of time or permanently (depending on severity).

Permanent ban — irreversible blocking of the account for the most serious violations.

1. General provisions

1.1. These Rules apply to all Game Chats: lobby, in-game, personal and group.

1.2. Communication is only allowed under your own account.

1.3. It is allowed to address participants by nickname or name (if the participant does not object).

1.4. Only one account per Player is allowed.

1.5. Supported chat languages: English, Russian, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Indonesian. Players may also communicate in other languages — this is not prohibited, but the Administration does not guarantee proper moderation of messages in unsupported languages.

1.6. Ignorance of the Rules does not exempt from responsibility.

2. Moderation of Chats and communication with the Game Administration

2.1. The Game Administration is not responsible for the content of player messages, but, as far as possible, suppresses violations.

2.2. Order in the chat is maintained by administrators and moderators.

2.3. In case of violations of these Rules or the laws of the Player’s country, the Administration may restrict or block access temporarily or permanently.

2.4. The Administration may terminate excessively long or provocative discussions.

2.5. The severity of violations and measures of influence (type/duration of block) are determined by the Administration, based on these Rules and the nature of the violation.

3. The following are prohibited in Chat:

3.1. Registering more than one account per Player (including after a block).

3.2. Sending messages from someone else’s account.

3.3. Profanity, disguised obscene language, prison slang — in chats, nicknames, or private messages.

3.4. Threats of violence, bullying, insults towards family members.

3.5. Publishing knowingly false information about the Administration.

3.6. Threats against the Administration.

3.7. Topics of modern politics and other deliberately conflict-provoking topics.

3.8. Materials directly or indirectly related to terrorist organizations, persons convicted by international tribunals; Nazi symbols, terms, designations, and names of corresponding historical figures.

3.9. Direct or indirect promotion/mention of drugs.

3.10. Distribution of unlicensed content, calls to break Game Rules, “cracks”, “warez”, “noCD”, etc.

3.11. Advertising in any form: links, referral links, spam, open promotion of other games/companies (except pre-approved fan resources).

3.12. Trading accounts/characters, “leveling” for real money.

3.13. Sharing information about cheats, exploits, vulnerabilities; use of malicious tools. Found vulnerabilities must be reported to the developer via e-mail or Discord.

3.14. Meaningless or repetitive messages (flood).

3.15. Begging (requests for money, game assets, etc.).

3.16. Publishing personal information of chat participants or correspondence with the Administration.

3.17. Sexualized content, including hints and texts with explicit/implicit sexual overtones (including in nicknames).

3.18. Insults and obscene language in any form.

3.19. Distribution of personal data of minors (e.g. phone numbers). This is to protect privacy.

3.20. Other actions considered violations at the discretion of the Administration.

3.21. Strictly prohibited: Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE):
— any materials, images, or discussions of sexual exploitation of minors;
— sharing/requesting intimate materials involving persons under 18;
— grooming and other attempts to involve children in sexual activities;
— any behavior sexualizing or endangering children.
Violation will result in immediate permanent ban and may be reported to relevant authorities.

4. Recommendations

4.1. Be respectful; differences of opinion are not grounds for insults.

4.2. If you notice a violation, use the in-game report instead of public arguments; attach a screenshot as evidence.

5. Other

5.1. The Administration may change these Rules, notifying Players through announcements in the game, on social media, or Discord. Continuing to use the Game means acceptance of the changes.

6. Publicity of messages and chat moderation

6.1. All chat messages are public and visible to all participants.

6.2. The Administration and authorized moderators have full and unrestricted access to chat messages for moderation and enforcement of these Rules.

6.3. By sending a message, the Player unconditionally agrees that it may be reviewed and analyzed by the Administration for moderation and security purposes.

6.4. The Administration and moderators may delete, edit, or otherwise act upon messages violating these Rules.

6.5. The Player acknowledges that there is no expectation of privacy regarding chat messages; such messages may be used for safety, quality improvement, or other lawful purposes.

7. Blocking terms

7.1. In most cases, a block lasts until the Player submits an unblock request (via e-mail snowbatstudio@gmail.com or Snow Bat social networks) and it is reviewed.

7.2. In CSAE cases, blocking is permanent and non-reviewable.

By clicking "OK" you agree to follow the chat and game rules described in this text.
