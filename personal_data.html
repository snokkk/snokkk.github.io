Privacy Policy (Account & Chat)
Last updated: 26 July 2025

Welcome to Imposter 3D: Online Horror!

This Policy explains how IP Demkin Danila (Snow Bat) (“we”, “us”, “Operator”) collects and uses the personal data you provide when you create or use a registered account in the game and when you participate in in-game chat.

1. Definitions

  • Personal data – information that identifies or can identify you.
  • Guest – player without a registered account (quick-chat only).
  • Registered user – player who creates an account through Firebase Authentication.

2. What data we collect and why

CategorySpecific dataPurpose
Account identifiersNickname, Firebase UID, e-mail / auth token*Create & protect account, display name in matches
Game progressCurrent level, in-game currency, settingsSave and restore your progress
Technical dataLast login time, game version, IP address, device ID (generated by Firebase/Photon)Security, anti-abuse, troubleshooting
Chat contentMessages and reportsModeration, safety, enforcement of rules

* We do not collect passwords; authentication is handled by Firebase.

3. Legal bases

We process data only when at least one legal basis applies:

  • Agreement (contract) – to provide the service you request (account, cloud save, chat).
  • Consent – you tick “Accept” before creating an account; guests stay anonymous.
  • Legitimate interest – ensure security, prevent fraud and toxicity.

4. Children & age limits

  • Creating an account is allowed only for users who confirm they are 13 years or older (or the age of digital consent in your country).
  • Players under this age must play as Guests; quick-chat limits free text.
  • We plan to introduce a birth-date check and parental-consent flow; until then, parental supervision is advised.

5. Who else processes the data

  • Google Firebase (USA/EU) – authentication, cloud save, crash logs
  • Photon PUN 2 (Germany) – real-time multiplayer; receives PlayerID & IP for matchmaking
  • Yandex Cloud (Russia) – stores chat logs and moderation reports

These providers act on our instructions and may not use your data for their own marketing.

6. Storage periods

  • Account & progress – while the account exists.
  • Chat logs & reports – up to 365 days then auto-deleted, unless needed to investigate rule violations.
  • Back-ups – retained 7 days before secure deletion.

7. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • request a copy of your data;
  • correct inaccurate data;
  • delete the account (see §8);
  • withdraw consent and restrict processing.

Requests are processed within 30 days: snowbatstudio@gmail.com.

8. How to delete your account

  1. Open Online > Account > Account Settings
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm; the data disappears from the game immediately and is erased from our servers within 30 days.

9. Security measures

  • Traffic encrypted via TLS 1.3
  • Account tokens stored with industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • Strict access control & automated intrusion monitoring

No internet system is 100 % secure; we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

10. International transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located in different jurisdictions. We rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards to protect it.

11. Policy changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes are announced in-game or on the website. Continued use after the update means acceptance of the new terms.

12. Contacts

E-mail: snowbatstudio@gmail.com

By clicking “Accept” in the game you confirm that you have read, understood and agree with these terms and that you are at least 13 years old (or have obtained parental consent).

If you do not agree, please exit the game and uninstall it.
